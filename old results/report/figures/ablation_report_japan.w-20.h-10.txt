Component Importance Analysis
----------------------------

1. Progressive Prediction Module (PPM): Enables region-aware multi-step forecasting with refinement
   Impact when removed: 3.60% RMSE degradation

2. Efficient Adaptive Graph Attention Module (EAGAM): Captures spatial dependencies between regions with linear complexity
   Impact when removed: 2.85% RMSE degradation

3. Dilated Multi-Scale Temporal Module (DMTM): Processes time-series patterns at different temporal resolutions
   Impact when removed: 0.68% RMSE degradation

Conclusion
----------

The ablation study demonstrates that the PPM component contributes most significantly to model performance, with its removal resulting in a 3.60% degradation in RMSE.

While all components contribute positively to the model's predictive capability, the DMTM component shows the least individual impact with a 0.68% RMSE degradation when removed.

The full MSAGAT-Net model with all components intact demonstrates superior performance across all metrics, confirming the value of the complete architecture design.
